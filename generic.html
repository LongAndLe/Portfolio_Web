Khai Hoan Land Corporation Analysis

I, Potential:
+ Top real estate brokerage market share in Ho Chi Minh City and Southern Vietnam thanks to employee policy "No salary - high commission"
+ Owns 800 ha land bank - Exclusive cooperation with T&T in real estate distribution
+ Concentrated shareholder structure
+ Low Total Debt/Equity ratio compared to other companies in the industry
II, Valuation:
+ Valuation by P/E. Compare with NLG, KDH, and PDR in the primary real estate segment, CRE in the secondary real estate, and real estate brokerage segment
III, Risks:
+ Leadership holds controlling power => Continuously approve private placement of 45% of outstanding shares in 2021, and 2022 => Severe EPS dilution
+ The debt of 2,500 billion VND in bonds with interest rates higher than the industry average => Doubts about cash flow
